.TASK

Recreate a full visual identity for a FantaCycling team: logo design, app and website UI, social media content, advertising, merchandise, and business cards. The previous F logo becomes a cyclist mid-sprint. The Pantani palette borrows from race routes and jersey traditions. The illustrations give the same feeling as the sport: being always in motion.

.TASK

Create a visual identity for the Meet Archive, a collection of tech objects spanning from different decades each one a small piece of cultural memory. The central concept is the technological fossil. Every object is presented through an animated poster: buried under dense, sand-like particles that accumulate with time.
