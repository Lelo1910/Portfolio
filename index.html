Hello! My name is Eleonora Benso but I'm know as Lelo in real life! I'm an Italian communication designer currently based in Salzburg, Austria. I have worked previously in various city such as Rome and Milan. I specialize in motion design, video editing, photography and campaign design.

I am passionate about telling stories in whatever medium I can. Turning abstract concepts into realities that multiple people can share and connect emotionally to. I am fascinated by constantly exploring new ways to portray different narratives in my projects.
